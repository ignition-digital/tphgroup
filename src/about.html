About Us

Driven by Vision,
Defined by Impact.

TPH Group is a dynamic and diversified company with core ventures in property development and pyrotechnics. Our journey began with a simple vision — to create impactful developments and unforgettable experiences that contribute to a brighter, more inspired world. Today, that vision drives everything we do, guiding our growth across industries and markets.

In the field of property development, TPH Group is committed to delivering high-quality residential, commercial, and mixed-use projects that add lasting value to communities. We emphasize thoughtful design, sustainability, and long-term functionality in every development. From master-planned neighborhoods to commercial hubs, we build with purpose and precision, meeting the evolving needs of modern lifestyles and businesses.

Alongside our real estate ventures, TPH Group is also a leading name in the pyrotechnics wholesale industry through our dedicated brand, Bright Future. We supply professional-grade fireworks and special effects to event organizers, retailers, and entertainment companies. With a strong emphasis on safety, compliance, and creative impact, our pyrotechnics division helps turn celebrations into lasting memories — from local events to grand-scale productions.

Our strength lies in our ability to bridge diverse sectors with a unified approach to excellence, innovation, and reliability. Whether we're laying the foundations of a new community or illuminating the skies, TPH Group operates with the same core principles: professionalism, trust, and a commitment to delivering value that lasts.

As we look toward the future, we remain focused on expanding our footprint, nurturing strategic partnerships, and embracing new opportunities across industries. At TPH Group, we don’t just build structures or shows — we build legacies.
